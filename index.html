    About Us

    National Catering Services & Foodstuff LLC (NCSF)

    National Catering Services & Foodstuff LLC (NCSF), is one of the leading catering and facility management service provider that specializes in providing catering and facility management services to corporate, governmental, and non-governmental private sectors as well as managing camp facilities to contractors working in the oil and gas industry with our extensive experience in working with ADNOC Group in both their onshore and offshore projects. We are based in Abu Dhabi and have been at the cutting edge of the catering and FM industry for the past 16 years.

    We provide high-quality, cost-effective catering, and support services to clients with a diverse range of nationalities in the Region located either in city based sites, mid-sea oil rigs, desert camps, power plants and military institutions. NCSF has its business operations across all seven emirates of the U.A.E including abroad in the Republic of IRAQ were we have provided USAF, ASARS, and ITT the necessary life support services for their Military and air force bases in Taji located some 20 kilometers north of the city of Baghdad IRAQ. NCSF Head office is located Office No. 904, Al Ferdous Tower, Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Street (Salam Street), Abu Dhabi United Arab Emirates that accommodates the General and Senior Operational Management, as well as personnel, accounting & operational/logistical support departments of the Company.
